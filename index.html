About

Rey Galindo was born in Mexico City, Mexico. He has lived in Cleveland, Ohio most of his life. He graduated from The Ohio State University in 1981 in Economics and also studied at the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México in Mexico City.

Rey began his career in the restaurant business in 1987 as Chef and co-owner of Luchita's Mexican Restaurant which has been in business for over thirty-five years. Rey has been an intricate part of the Hispanic community in the Cleveland area with experience with the Esperanza organization as a board member and a volunteer to help underprivileged students gain grants for higher education.

He retired from the restaurant business in 2013 and started his career in Financial Services in 2014 with Morin-Levine Finacial Services. Rey left Morin-Levine Finacial Services in 2018 to join the Kestler Financial Group to provide you with the best service in Finance and Insurance.
